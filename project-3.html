Cryptique Hash

I developed an application available on Google Play that addresses a challenge posed by a friend. The goal was to communicate in a cryptic manner without being understood by others. The application encrypts plain text and decrypts encrypted text, ensuring private communication.

The security of the application relies on the use of a password and a password salt. The use of long and uncommon passwords, combined with the use of salt and increased complexity in the code, enhances the overall security of the application.
